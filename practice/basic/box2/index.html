A Study Hall joint

Thick Pickle is a Study Hall project. We're a design studio out of Oakland, CA.This whole website is an exploration into a project that really should be. We're quite passionate about food here, and we've been known to ferment a hot sauce or sous vide a steakums.

We're actually half serious about this whole endeavor. Honestly speaking, what started as a humble and fun side project to make a funny website about pickles has morphed into more than we ever thought it would.

We've had countless meetings with food scientists, michelin chefs, expert picklers and more. This website is a testing ground to validate the collective need, for huge, juicy big boys (pickles, you perv).
