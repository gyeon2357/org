We collect and analyze data on the hotel's location, market demographics, and consumption levels to define the target persona. We then use quantitative and qualitative data from competitors to determine correlations between various variables and sales, proposing effective strategies and positioning. Additionally, based on positioning and competitive advantage, we set appropriate pricing and estimate profitability to evaluate the feasibility of the business.

Based on these keywords, we create a brand story to strengthen the connection between the customer and the brand. We develop layout plans that reflect the space development concept and create architectural plans based on regulatory requirements.
